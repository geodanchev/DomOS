# DomOS Product Principles

DomOS is a legal-first SaaS platform for managing residential buildings under Bulgarian ZUES.

## Core Principles

1. Legal correctness before convenience.
2. Auditability before speed.
3. Structured workflows before free-form actions.
4. Deterministic backend logic before AI-generated assumptions.
5. Multi-tenant SaaS architecture from the beginning.
6. Simple MVP first, scalable architecture second.
7. Never delete legally relevant information.
8. Every critical action must be traceable.
9. Users are non-technical; workflows must be clear and guided.
10. Apply SOLID principles when designing modules and services.

## Product Goal

DomOS should reduce or eventually replace the need for a traditional building manager by providing trusted workflows for:

- building registry
- resident and ownership management
- meetings
- voting
- decisions
- documents
- repairs
- obligations
- payments
- audit history

## Critical Rule

Assume that every important action may later need to be used as evidence in a legal dispute.
